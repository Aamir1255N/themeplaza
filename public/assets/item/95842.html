Barbie Mermaid Tale Surfer

Barbie Mermaid Tale Surfer tropical grily y2k

Catch a wave of tropical nostalgia with this Barbie Mermaid Tale Surfer 3DS theme! The top screen features Barbie and her friends riding sparkling waves, surrounded by vibrant floral patterns, dolphins, and a glittering ocean backdrop. The bottom screen showcases a serene tropical seascape with bright pink and orange hibiscus flowers floating against a dreamy turquoise sky. Icons and folders are styled in pastel aqua and bubblegum pink, perfectly capturing the Y2K girly beach vibe. This theme is ideal for fans of sunny tropical adventures, mermaid magic, and Barbie's fun, carefree style!
